Change from within

C4E promotes change by making the community take ownership. By supporting our projects, you partake in the bottom-up system that emphasizes the need for the community people to be at the center of their own success.

Supporting a C4E learner to get relevant skills cannot be in vain. Learners’ projects that include farming and artifacts do not only help community members to earn a living, but it also works as motivation and foundation for a better story. You can be part of that story.
