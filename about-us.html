Welcome to C4E

“Without education, your children can never really meet the challenges they will face. So it is very important to give children education and explain that they should play a role for their country” Nelson Mandela.

Care for Education is promoting a bottom-up system of learning where skills are community-centered, and learner-friendly to ensure relevant and updated education outcomes. Our aim is to make certain that young people are able to use education skills acquired in an influential way that helps both them and the entire community to have a successful development. Among the skills promoted, we emphasize on creating a community with learners that have self-awareness, talent identification and development, cultural and ethical values, reading culture support, future planning, small business knowledge, as well as livelihoods support.
