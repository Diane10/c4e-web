Launching an online learning platform. Cultural Academic Series(CAS)

C4E will be providing lessons that are essential for community progress through this website. In the foremost stages, we shall be launching the Cultural Academic Series (CAS) with the aim of helping people to access educational content on the Rwandan culture.

About C4E

Care for Education (C4E), is a youth led project with an aim of helping the young generation in rural areas of Rwanda to merge skills they get from education to the needs in their communities.
