C4E 3Cs Model

C4E 3Cs model is a strategy through which we foster community participation and development in Cognition,Commerce and Culture.

Cognition

We are developing competitiveness among learners through reading, writing, and public expression. We need a generation that owns and shares their content.

Commerce

Our trainees get involved in development projects and get exposure to other various economic opportunities that help them to earn a living with acquired level of competitiveness.
